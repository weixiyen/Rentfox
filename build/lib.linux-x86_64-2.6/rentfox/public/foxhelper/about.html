About Rentfox

Rentfox is a web based software company that helps property managers and landlords efficiently manage their rental properties.

We like to compete on ease-of-use and customer service.

Our goal is to create software that helps landlords find and retain tenants, while providing tools to help maintain a good relationship between them.

Rentfox was founded by 3 U.C. Berkeley graduates in 2008 and operates out of San Francisco, CA.
